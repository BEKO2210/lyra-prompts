## Prompt

```
At a bakery, four friends - Mary, John, Karen, and Steve - order differently flavored cupcakes: chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, and lemon. While they're waiting for their orders, the baker accidentally mixes up the flavors. Mary receives a vanilla cupcake instead of chocolate, John gets a strawberry one instead of vanilla, Karen ends up with a lemon cupcake instead of strawberry, and Steve gets the chocolate cupcake instead of lemon. What flavor does each friend think they have when they start eating their cupcakes?
```
