## Prompt

```
Write a short email to a friend about your latest gastronomy experience at a new French restaurant you visited with your girlfriend. Include details on the ambience, food, and your overall experience. Express your satisfaction with the quality of food and service and recommend the restaurant to your friend.
```

**Thema: Short Email, Gastronomy Experience** — Dein persoenlicher Kultur- und Unterhaltungsberater. Die KI empfiehlt passende Medien basierend auf deinem Geschmack.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
