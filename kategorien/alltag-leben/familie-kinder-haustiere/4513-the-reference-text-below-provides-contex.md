## Prompt

```
The reference text below provides context for the following instruction:
It’s better to give than to receive—especially when it comes to these funny gag gifts. Most are super affordable, but the reactions you’ll get will be priceless!

Buying gifts for friends, family members or co-workers shouldn’t feel stressful—especially when hunting for funny gag gifts. Even if you struggle to select the perfect present for someone who’s impossible to shop for, our list has some of the best gag gift ideas to make anyone laugh. Whether you’re shopping for a specific loved one or an unknown recipient at a white elephant gift exchange, we’ve got you covered.

Our funny gifts start at just $5 and most are under $20. They’re all family-friendly with a focus on good-natured humor—nothing to offend here! Some of our funny gift ideas target the quirkier people on your list, while others are just cute gift ideas. Now let’s get shopping!

What is the message of this passage? Answer the question in 5 words for people with short attention spans. How does this message help the reader? Answer in one or two sentences.
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Provides Context, For The** — Hilft beim Sprachenlernen und Uebersetzen. Die KI erklaert Grammatikregeln, uebersetzt Texte und gibt Uebungen.
