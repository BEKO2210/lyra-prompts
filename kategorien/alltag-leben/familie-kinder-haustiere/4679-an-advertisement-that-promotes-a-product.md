## Prompt

```
Write an advertisement that promotes a product for a niche market of environmentally conscious consumers who value sustainable materials and ethical labor practices. The advertisement must include a table comparing the carbon footprint and water usage of the product to its competitors. Additionally, the advertisement must be written in both English and Spanish, and must include a code snippet demonstrating the product's compatibility with popular home automation systems. Inquiries about the product's supply chain and sourcing of materials must also be addressed in depth, including information about fair trade certifications and the use of renewable energy sources in production. Finally, the advertisement must specifically target individuals who prioritize eco-friendliness in their purchasing decisions, and must use language that appeals to this audience.
```
