## Prompt

```
Design a pair of sneakers that must meet the following requirements: 
- Must be made from sustainable materials and have a minimal carbon footprint
- Must be suitable for both indoor and outdoor activities 
- Must have a unique design that incorporates elements from at least two different cultures 
- Must have a built-in fitness tracker that can monitor the wearer's heart rate, steps taken, and calories burned 
- Must be available in sizes ranging from US men's 4 to 16 and women's 5 to 12 
- Must be compatible with both iOS and Android devices 
- Must have a custom lacing system that allows for easy adjustments and provides maximum support 
- Must be able to withstand extreme weather conditions, including heavy rain and snow 
- Must have a removable insole that can be easily cleaned and replaced 
- Must be priced competitively with other high-end sneakers on the market 
Additionally, please provide a detailed breakdown of the materials used in the sneakers, including their environmental impact and sourcing information. Please also include a 3D rendering of the sneakers, as well as a technical drawing with measurements and specifications. Finally, please provide a sample code for an app that can be used to sync with the fitness tracker and display the wearer's activity data in real-time.
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Sustainable Materials, Minimal Carbon** — Gibt praktische Tipps fuer einen nachhaltigeren Lebensstil. Die KI liefert faktenbasierte Informationen zu Umweltthemen.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Details an deine Beduerfnisse an.

## Variationen

- Frage nach konkreten Massnahmen fuer deinen Alltag
- Nenne dein Budget fuer nachhaltige Alternativen
- Bitte um einen Vergleich der Umweltauswirkungen
- Frage nach lokalen Initiativen und Moeglichkeiten
