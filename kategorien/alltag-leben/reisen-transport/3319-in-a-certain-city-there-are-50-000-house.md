## Prompt

```
In a certain city, there are 50,000 households. Of these, 60% own at least one car, and 40% own at least one bicycle. What is the minimum possible number of vehicles (cars and bicycles combined) owned by households in this city?
```

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn direkt in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Platzhalter und Details an deine Situation an.

- **Tipp:** Fuer bessere Ergebnisse, fuege spezifische Details zu deiner Situation hinzu
