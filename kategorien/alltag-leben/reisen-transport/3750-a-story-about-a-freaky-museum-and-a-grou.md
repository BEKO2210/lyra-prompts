## Prompt

```
Write a story about a freaky museum and a group of four travelers who embark on a road trip. They decide to visit the museum, which is ran by Captain Spaulding, a grotesque clown. He tells them a tale about Dr. Satan, a mad doctor known for his horrific experiments. Inspired by the tale, they ask the clown how they can find where Dr. Satan used to perform his experiments and the clown provides them a map to an abandoned tree where his victims were buried. On their journey, they meet a strange family who invite them into their strange, eccentric home. The atmosphere grows more and more sinister as they spend time with this bizarre family and their motives are revealed. As terror unfolds, the travelers find themselves in a fight for survival.
```

## Anwendung

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn direkt in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Platzhalter und Details an deine Situation an.

- **Kategorie:** Alltag & Leben

- **Tipp:** Fuer bessere Ergebnisse, fuege spezifische Details zu deiner Situation hinzu
