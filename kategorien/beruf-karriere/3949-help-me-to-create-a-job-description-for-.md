## Prompt

```
Please help me to create a job description for a Transformation Manager for Comunidad Judía de Madrid. 
Key responsibilities
- Leading large-scale change in an organization
- Working with a variety of stakeholders and aligning different departments / teams
- Implementing new strategies, processes, systems and standards
- Orchestrating positive impact covering all transformation initiatives

Selected tasks
- Developing a case for each initiative and presenting it to senior leadership for approval
- Monitoring the progress of each initiative throughout its life cycle to ensure that deadlines are met and objectives are achieved
- Developing detailed implementation plans
- Coordinating with teams and other stakeholders
- Conducting training sessions
- Determining how to allocate resources to meet project goals / deadlines
- Working with external partners
- Analyzing data to determine where improvements can be made to increase efficiency or effectiveness of processes
- Establishing communication plans
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Job Description, Transformation Manager** — Spart Zeit beim Verfassen wichtiger Nachrichten. Die KI formuliert professionelle E-Mails und Briefe im richtigen Ton.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Details an deine Beduerfnisse an.

## Variationen

- Beschreibe die Beziehung zum Empfaenger (Chef, Kunde, Kollege)
- Gib den gewuenschten Ton an: formell, freundlich, oder bestimmt
- Nenne die Kernbotschaft in einem Satz
- Frage nach einer kuerzeren/laengeren Version
