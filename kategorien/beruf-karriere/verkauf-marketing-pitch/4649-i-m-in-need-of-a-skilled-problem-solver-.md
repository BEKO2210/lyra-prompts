## Prompt

```
I'm in need of a skilled problem solver to assist me with a complex puzzle involving a miniature Christmas tree product. I possess data on its sales and revenue from both department stores and online platforms. I require a pricing strategy that can optimize revenue while considering production costs, competitor pricing, and demand elasticity. Furthermore, I require an intricate formula that can calculate the optimal price for each distribution channel. On top of that, I need a comprehensive analysis of the influence of promotional campaigns on sales and revenue so that I can adjust the pricing strategy accordingly. Can you lend me your expertise to solve this challenging and demanding problem of devising the best pricing and promotional strategy that can maximize revenue for this product?
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Skilled Problem, Complex Puzzle** — Unterstuetzt dich bei beruflichen Herausforderungen. Passe den Prompt an deine Branche und Position an.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Details an deine Beduerfnisse an.
