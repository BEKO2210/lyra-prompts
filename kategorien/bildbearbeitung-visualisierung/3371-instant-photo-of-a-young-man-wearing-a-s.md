- Aendere die Perspektive: "from above", "low angle", "close-up", "wide shot"
- Aendere die Beleuchtung: "golden hour", "studio lighting", "neon", "moody"
- Fuege Negativ-Prompts hinzu: "no blur, no artifacts, no watermark"
