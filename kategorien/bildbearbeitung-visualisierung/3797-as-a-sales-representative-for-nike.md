## Prompt

```
As a sales representative for Nike, I want to inform customers about the availability of our new product - Nike shoes. Can you please provide me with an email template that would be suitable for sending to potential customers? The email should include details about the product's features and benefits, as well as pricing and availability. It should also include a call to action that encourages customers to make a purchase. The email should be in HTML format and be visually appealing, with images of the product included. Thank you for your assistance.
```
