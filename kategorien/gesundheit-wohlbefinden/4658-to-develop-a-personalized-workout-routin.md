First, determine which genetic markers correspond to the best workout routine by referring to the table below. Then, use your logical skills to create a customized routine that takes into account both the individual's genetic makeup and the optimal workout routine suggested by the DNA analysis. To do this, you must consider various factors such as the person's fitness level, medical history, and personal preferences. Only after successfully completing these steps can you design an effective and personalized workout routine that achieves the desired results. Good luck!
