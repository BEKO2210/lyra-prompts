## Prompt

```
Detective Smith is currently faced with the challenge of identifying the thief behind a series of valuable art robberies that have occurred across the city. The robberies have taken place at different locations and have resulted in the loss of valuable art pieces. However, Detective Smith has narrowed down his list of suspects to five individuals: a wealthy art collector, a gallery owner, an art dealer, a famous artist, and a museum curator. To make things more complicated, Detective Smith can only identify the culprit by using a complex formula that involves three variables. The variables include the number of letters in the suspect's first name, the number of letters in their last name, and the number of vowels in their full name. Can you help Detective Smith by utilizing the formula to deduce which of the five suspects is the real culprit behind these art thefts?
```
