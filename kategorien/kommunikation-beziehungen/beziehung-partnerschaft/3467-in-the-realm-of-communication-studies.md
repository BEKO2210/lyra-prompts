## Prompt

In the realm of communication studies, media studies play a crucial role in understanding how information is disseminated and interpreted. Media outlets, including newspapers, television, and social media platforms, have a significant influence on public opinion. Interpersonal communication, on the other hand, focuses on the exchange of information between individuals and the impact of this exchange on relationships. Public speaking, a subset of interpersonal communication, involves delivering information to an audience in a structured, deliberate manner intended to inform, influence, or entertain. Given this, which aspect of communication studies would be most relevant in studying the impact of fake news on public opinion?

A. Interpersonal Communication
B. Media Studies
C. Public Speaking
D. Non-Verbal Communication
