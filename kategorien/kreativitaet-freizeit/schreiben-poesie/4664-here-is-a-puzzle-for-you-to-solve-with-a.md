## Prompt

```
Here is a puzzle for you to solve with an added requirement! 
Imagine a young man named Kamal who lived an unhappy life. He had few friends and was stuck in a mundane routine. However, one day, something caught his attention while he was scrolling through his social media feed. He stumbled upon an old friend's post about a charity event. What was it that caught Kamal's attention on social media that made him want to attend the charity event? Your added requirement is to provide at least three different possible scenarios that could have caught Kamal's attention. 
When he attended the event, he met a group of enthusiastic volunteers who shared their stories of making a difference in people's lives. They inspired Kamal to join them, and he started devoting his free time to volunteering. As he continued to volunteer, Kamal's network of friends grew. He was now surrounded by positive and like-minded people who appreciated his efforts. What did Kamal discover about himself as he started volunteering, and how did it impact his life? 
If you can solve this puzzle, you will have uncovered the inspiring story of Kamal's transformation from an unhappy life to a fulfilling one.
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Puzzle For, Added Requirement** — Perfekt fuer kreative Schreibprojekte und Inspiration. Die KI generiert Texte in verschiedenen Genres und Stilen.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Details an deine Beduerfnisse an.

## Variationen

- Gib Genre und Stimmung an (lustig, dunkel, romantisch)
- Nenne eine gewuenschte Wortanzahl
- Frage nach alternativen Enden oder Perspektiven
- Bitte die KI, im Stil eines bestimmten Autors zu schreiben
