## Prompt

```
Write a sales email to a potential buyer. In the email, you should explain why your products are superior to competitors’ products and should be given preference. Additionally, include details about your company’s history and why that should give the buyer confidence in your products. Close the email by giving the buyer a clear next step, such as scheduling a call or visit to your showroom.
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Sales Email, Potential Buyer** — Dein persoenlicher Nachhilfelehrer fuer jedes Thema. Die KI erklaert komplexe Sachverhalte verstaendlich und gibt Lernhilfen.
