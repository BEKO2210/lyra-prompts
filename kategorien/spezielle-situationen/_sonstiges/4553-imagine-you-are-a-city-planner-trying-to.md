## Prompt

```
Imagine you are a city planner trying to decide whether to build a new metropolis or expand an existing rural area. You must consider three factors that would make a metropolis more advantageous than a rural area. 
First, think about the economic benefits. If you build a metropolis, what would be the minimum number of people needed to make it economically viable? 
Next, consider the social impact of living in a metropolis. How would it compare to living in a rural area in terms of community involvement and sense of belonging? 
Lastly, think about the environmental impact of a metropolis versus a rural area. What steps would you need to take to minimize the negative impact on the environment, and how would it compare to a rural area in terms of sustainability? 
Use your reasoning skills to determine the three most significant advantages of residing in a metropolis over a rural area, taking into account cultural, economic, social, and environmental aspects.
```
