## Prompt

```
Imagine you're the owner of a small business and you're struggling to attract customers. You know that traditional advertising methods just aren't cutting it anymore, but you're not sure what to do next. Here's a puzzle for you: what strategy can you implement that will not only bring in new customers, but also establish your brand and reputation in your industry? The answer lies in a technique that involves creating and sharing valuable content that resonates with your target audience. By consistently delivering helpful and engaging content through blog posts, social media, and other channels, you can build trust and credibility with your customers, ultimately leading to increased sales and long-term loyalty. So, what is this puzzle-solving strategy called? Hint: it starts with "C" and ends with "marketing." Can you crack the code?
```
