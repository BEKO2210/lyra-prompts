## Prompt

```
For your next project, you want to create a mobile app that displays a web page with more complex structure and hierarchy. In addition to the header, body, and footer elements, you want to incorporate a sidebar with additional links and categories. The header should have a logo image on the left and a navigation menu on the right with five links: "Home", "About", "Contact", "Services", and "Blog". The body should have three sections: a "Featured" section, a "Latest News" section, and a "Popular Posts" section. The "Featured" section should have a title, an image, a description, and a button to read more. The "Latest News" section should have a title and a list of five news articles with titles, dates, and a button to read more. The "Popular Posts" section should have a title and a list of five blog posts with titles, authors, dates, and a button to read more. The sidebar should have a search bar, a list of categories, a list of tags, and a section for social media links. Finally, the footer should have a copyright notice, a link to your company's website, and a brief summary of your company's mission and values.
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Next Project, Mobile App** — Unterstuetzt bei der Geschaeftsplanung und Unternehmensgruendung. Die KI liefert strukturierte Analysen und Strategievorschlaege.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Details an deine Beduerfnisse an.
