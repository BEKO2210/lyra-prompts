## Prompt

```
You have been chosen to lead a team of explorers on a dangerous mission to a remote planet. However, each team member has a unique limitation that must be taken into account when selecting the team. The explorers and their limitations are:
- Biochemist: highly allergic to alien plant life
- Geologist: struggles to operate in low gravity environments
- Linguist: unable to communicate with non-humanoid life forms
- Engineer: prone to motion sickness
- Botanist: cannot tolerate extreme temperatures
Your goal is to create a team that can successfully navigate and collect data on the planet. Use the following code to assign a score to each explorer based on their skills and limitations:
```
skills = {"Biochemist": 9, "Geologist": 8, "Linguist": 7, "Engineer": 6, "Botanist": 5}
limitations = {"Biochemist": 3, "Geologist": 4, "Linguist": 8, "Engineer": 7, "Botanist": 6}
```
Arrange the explorers in a particular order based on their overall score, calculated by subtracting their limitation score from their skill score. Use a mathematical formula to calculate the product of the positions of the top two explorers in the list. What is the resulting product?
```

## Anwendung

**Thema: Dangerous Mission, Remote Planet** — Gibt praktische Tipps fuer einen nachhaltigeren Lebensstil. Die KI liefert faktenbasierte Informationen zu Umweltthemen.

Kopiere den Prompt und fuege ihn in ChatGPT, Claude oder Gemini ein.
Passe die Details an deine Beduerfnisse an.
